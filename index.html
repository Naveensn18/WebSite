MullayyanaGiri

Mullayyanagiri is the highest peak in Karnataka,India.Mullayyanagiri is located in the Chandra Dhrona Hill Ranges of the Western Ghats of Chikkamagaluru Taluk. With a height of 1,925 metres, it is the highest peak in Karnataka and also the 23rd highest peak in Western Ghats

Devaramane

Devaramane offers various varieties that make it an ideal journeying spot for the two novices and no-nonsense travelers. There are a few hillocks to climb, the Ettinabujha-Bulls Hump-back, overshadows the other tops nearby. From a stature of 3000 ft, the thunder of the stream streaming underneath can be heard.
